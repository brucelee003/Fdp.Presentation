The R.A.F.T. Formula

A structured approach to building better prompts by defining four key components

R

Role

Who should the AI act as? Define persona, expertise level, and tone.

Example "Act as a Senior NASA Engineer..."
A

Action

What task should be performed? Use strong directive verbs.

Example "Explain the propulsion system..."
F

Format

How should the output be structured?

Example "In a 3-column table..."
T

Target

Who is the audience?

Example "For middle school students..."
TIP: Combine all four elements in every prompt for optimal results
