Getting Started

NotebookLM is unique because it is "grounded" in your documents. Unlike ChatGPT, which uses general internet knowledge, NotebookLM prioritizes the sources you upload.

Setup Time: < 2 Minutes
URL: notebooklm.google.com
Supports: PDF, Docs, Slides, URLs
PRIVACY FIRST

Your data stays private. NotebookLM does not use your personal uploads to train its global models.

1

Login & Access

Navigate to notebooklm.google and sign in with your Google Workspace or personal account.

2

Create Notebook

Click the large "New Notebook" box. Give it a title relevant to your course (e.g., "Thermodynamics 101").

3

Upload Sources

Select PDFs, Google Docs, or paste URLs. This creates the "brain" for your specific notebook.

4

Interact & Query

Start asking questions, request summaries, or click "Audio Overview" to generate a podcast.
