Skeleton-of-Thought (SoT)

Managing long-form generation by defining structure before content

1

Generate Skeleton

Ask the AI to draft a detailed outline with headings and bullet points first, without writing full paragraphs.

"Draft outline for..."
2

Expand Section-by-Section

Once the skeleton is approved, ask the AI to expand each bullet point into full content sequentially.

"Expand Section 1..."
3

Coherent Output

Result is a long-form document that stays strictly on topic without "hallucinating" or drifting away from the core thesis.

Final Document

KEY BENEFITS:

Controls Topic Drift
Enhances Coherence
