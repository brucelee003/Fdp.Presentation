ADVANCED PROMPTING

Advanced Technique:
Few-Shot Prompting

Move beyond simple instructions by providing examples to calibrate the AI model to your specific requirements and grading style.

The Goal: Calibration

"Teach" the AI your specific style, tone, and depth requirements before asking it to generate new content.

The Method: 2-3 Perfect Examples

Provide 2-3 "gold standard" examples (Input + Ideal Output) that reflect your exact grading rubric or desired format.

Pro Tip: Rationalize

Label examples clearly as EXAMPLE 1/2/3 and add a brief rationale explaining why the example is good.

// INSTRUCTION: Grade student responses based on clarity and technical accuracy.
