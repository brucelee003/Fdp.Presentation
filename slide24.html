How NotebookLM Works

The "Closed-Loop" Advantage: Generating answers grounded exclusively in your data.

1

Source Documents

Upload your PDFs, Google Docs, Slides, or text files. This forms your private "knowledge boundary."

2

NotebookLM Engine

The model analyzes ONLY your provided sources, creating a grounded index for retrieval.

3

Fact-Checked Output

Answers are generated with inline citations [1] that link directly back to the specific source passage.

GROUNDING GUARANTEE

Unlike ChatGPT, NotebookLM will not "hallucinate" outside facts. If it's not in your docs, it won't say it.
